Multiplication Quest

Restore the Number Kingdom by mastering math facts 1–10.

Student Safety: Do not use your real name. Choose a nickname like StarFox, MathDragon, or BlueComet. This game saves scores only on this device/browser.

Quest Lore

The Number Kingdom is protected by ten crystal gates. The bosses stole the multiplication sparks that power each gate. Win facts, earn coins, upgrade powers, and defeat each boss to restore fluency.

Game Rules

  • Answer multiplication facts from 1–10.
  • Each area ends with a boss battle.
  • After every question, choose: upgrade, next question, or main menu.
  • Wrong answers give feedback and do not collect personal information.
